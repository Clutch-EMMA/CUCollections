How We Calculate Your ROI

  • Charge-Off Reduction (Direct): Recovery improvement from accounts assigned directly to EMMA
  • Charge-Off Reduction (Indirect): Boost to human collector recovery efforts supported by EMMA
  • Headcount Avoidance: Labor cost savings by avoiding the need to hire additional collectors
  • Outsource Avoidance: Savings from reducing or eliminating third-party collection outsourcing
  • EMMA Platform Cost: Licensing and usage fees are deducted to reflect true net ROI
